GRAPHIC'S CARD

A graphics card processes images, videos, and animations. It improves visual performance, especially for gaming and editing. It has its own memory (VRAM) for faster processing. A good GPU makes graphics smoother and clearer.

MOTHERBOARD

The motherboard is the main circuit board of a computer. It connects all components like the CPU, RAM, storage, and graphics card. It allows communication between different parts of the system. Everything in the computer works through the motherboard.

POWER SUPPLY

The power supply unit provides electricity to all computer components. It converts AC power from the wall into usable DC power. It ensures stable and safe power flow. Without a PSU, the computer cannot function.
